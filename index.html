Engagement ring with lab-grown diamond

Diamonds grown in the lab are becoming more and more popular. What is the difference between synthetic diamonds and natural diamonds? Here is our response:

Synthesized diamonds have the same physical and chemical properties as natural diamonds. The necessary conditions required for a diamond to grow naturally have been recreated in the laboratory.

It takes weeks to grow a diamond in a laboratory, while natural diamonds take centuries to form.

Distinguishing a synthesized diamond from a natural diamond is not possible if you are not using laboratory equipment.

To evaluate synthesized diamonds, the same 4G method is used as for natural diamonds.

So, is the only difference between synthetic and natural diamonds the fact that some of them are created by nature, while others are created by men? Yes, but there is another fundamental difference - the price. Natural diamonds' price has been increasing throughout their history, and will keep doing so, because their resources are exhaustible. Synthetic diamonds on the contrary, will become cheaper as their production volumes increase.

So the fundamental difference is the price. Today we offer the best prices for jewelries with synthetic diamonds from 0.25 carat and up.
